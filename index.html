About Amardeep Foundation

Working for a better society since 2015.

Our Mission

Founded in 2015, the Amardeep Foundation is a non-governmental organization committed to fostering positive change within society. Our core mission is to empower individuals and communities through targeted initiatives in education, health, and social welfare.

We envision a world where every person has the opportunity to reach their full potential. Through dedication and community collaboration, we strive to address and overcome the challenges faced by the underprivileged.

Our Key Initiatives

Making a tangible difference in rural communities.

Book Distribution Program

We actively distribute educational books and learning materials in rural areas, ensuring that children have the resources they need to succeed in their studies and beyond.

Community Health

Organizing health camps and awareness programs to promote well-being and provide essential medical services to underserved populations.

Social Welfare

Implementing various social welfare projects aimed at improving living conditions and providing support to vulnerable groups within the community.

Our Team & Inspiration

In Loving Memory of Amar Bahadur Yadav

The Amardeep Foundation was established in the cherished memory of Shri Amar Bahadur Yadav. His spirit of compassion, dedication to education, and dream of a better society are the cornerstones of our mission. We are committed to carrying forward his legacy by lighting the lamp of knowledge and hope in every life we touch.
